About PICKLE

Pickle is the native liquidity incentive and governance token associated with Pickle Finance, an experiment in leveraging community yield farming to bring stable coins closer to their peg. Users of Pickle Finance can earn PICKLE by contributing liquidity to various stablecoin pools in the Pickle Farm interface. Holders of PICKLE can create and vote on governance proposals through the Pickle Finance governance interface.
